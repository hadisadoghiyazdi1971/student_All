OVERVIEW: The AGM-65K MAVERICK is a short range, EO/CCD guided, rocket powered, air-to-ground missile with a 136 kg penetrating warhead.

DETAILS: The AGM-65K Maverick is a tactical air-to-surface missile designed for use against hardened targets.  The CCD seeker allows the missile to operate in lower light levels than the earlier -65B.  

Weight:		306 kg
Length:		2.5 m
Diameter:	0.31 m
Wingspan:	0.72 m
Speed:		620 kts (1,150 km/hr)
Range:		0.05-8 nm (0.1-14 km).  
CEP:		1.5 m

NOTES: IOC 1990s.
