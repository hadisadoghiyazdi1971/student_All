OVERVIEW: The AN/AXQ-14 Datalink pod is two-way datalink that provides guidance to GBU-15 and AGM-130 series bombs.

DETAILS: The AN/AXQ-14 Datalink is a two-way communications system that transmits a television or IR image from a camera mounted in the GBU-15"glide bomb" or the AGM-130 powered bomb to the launching aircraft, as well as guidance controls from a human controller back to the bome, allowing it to be "steered" to the target.
 

NOTES: Entered service in 1982.
