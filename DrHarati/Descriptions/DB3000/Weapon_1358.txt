OVERVIEW: The APR-1 KONDOR is an airborne-delievered, rocket-powered ASW active/passive acoustic homing torpedo with an 80 kg. warhead.

DETAILS:  The APR-1 (Aviacionnaia Podvodnaia Raketa-1) is an rocket powered ASW torpedo. The range of the homing system was 1,400 yards (1,300 m). Could be used to depths of 1,400 feet (420 m).

Specifications:

Weight:	650 kg
Range: 	800 meters


NOTES:  IOC: 1960s.
