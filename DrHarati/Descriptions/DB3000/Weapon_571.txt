OVERVIEW: The The AN/ALQ-220 Organic Airborne and Surface Influence Sweep (OASIS) is a helicopter-towed minesweeping device.

DETAILS: Description: The AN/ALQ-220,  tailored for rotary wing aircraft. This system installed on US Navy MH-60S helicopters will allow commanders to perform rapid minesweeping missions.
