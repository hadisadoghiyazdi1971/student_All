OVERVIEW: Marconi Type S.244 in powerful Long-Range Heigh-Finding radar based on older Type S.239.

DETAILS: Provides height of the aircrafts for main 2D air search radars. Used in NATO NADGE radar network.

NOTES: Used by Air Forces in Denmark and Norway.
