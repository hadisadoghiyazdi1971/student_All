OVERVIEW: The LS-6-50 is a GPS/INS guided bomb glidebomb with a 22kg HE filler.


DETAILS:   The LS-6 designation guidance kit that is applied to a gravity bomb to provide increased accuracy and range. The kit includes a semi-active homing laser (SAL) for increased accuracy. It is intended to engage fixed-position ground targets. 


Weight: 	  	50 kg
Filling Weight:	  	22 kg
Range:			21nm @ 26,000ft. release; 32.3nm @32,800 ft release
Accuracy:		15 m CEP



NOTES: IOC: 2010 (approx)
