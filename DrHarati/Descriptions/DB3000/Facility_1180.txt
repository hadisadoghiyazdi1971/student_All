OVERVIEW: The COBRA Counter Battery Radar. COBRA is mounted on a cross-country mobile vehicle. 

DETAILS: The COBRA mission is to locate mortars, rocket launchers and artillery batteries and to provide information for countering their effectiveness.

NOTES: COBRA (COunter Battery RAdar) is a collaborative long-range battlefield radar programme between Germany, France and the United Kingdom. COBRA radar systems are in service with effect from 2005.
