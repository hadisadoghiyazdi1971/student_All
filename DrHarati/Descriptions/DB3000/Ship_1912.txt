OVERVIEW: The UDALOY I DDG is a large, gas-turbine powered ASW destroyer.

DETAILS:   Designed primarily as an anti-submarine warfare platform, with a long cruising range and underway replenishment capabilities, the Udaloy class ships provide support to surface task forces.  The UDALOY complements the Sovremenny class destroyers.  A total of twelve ships were built.

NOTES: Displacement: 6700 tons standard
       Speed: 33-35 knts
       Range: 2400 nm @ 32 knts; 10500 nm at 14 knts
       Complement: 300 (approx.)  

SOURCES:  Polmar, Norman, and Siegfried Breyer. Guide to the Soviet Navy. Annapolis, Md: Naval Institute Press, 1983, pg. 155-56 ; Federation of American Scientists. "Udaloy Class - Project 1155." Accessed November 12, 2013. http://www.fas.org/man/dod-101/sys/ship/row/rus/1155_1.htm
