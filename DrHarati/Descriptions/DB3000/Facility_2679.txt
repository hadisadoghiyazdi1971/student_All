OVERVIEW: The S-244 radar is a fixed height finding radar, operating in the NATO "E" band.  

DETAILS: The S-244 radar was a nodding-type height finding radar.


Specifications:

Frequency			2.7-3.3 GHz (E band (NATO),  S Band (IEEE))
Power output: 			3-5 kW (average); 3 MW (peak)
PRF:				200-500 pps
Range Resolution:		1700 ft at 150 nm
On/Off time: 			unknown min. 
Deploy/Stow time: 		N/A min
Maximum Range:			 km (range), km (altitude)
	  
 

NOTES:   Used in NATO NADGE radar network.
