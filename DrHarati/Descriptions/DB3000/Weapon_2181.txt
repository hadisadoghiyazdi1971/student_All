OVERVIEW: The A-22 Ogon OF-45 140mm rocket is a short-range, unguided solid fuel intended for use against area targets in littoral regions. It has a 5.65kg HE warhead.

DETAILS: 


Specifications:
 
Weight: 		   28 kg 
Length: 		   1.125 m 
Diameter: 		  140mm  
 Range:			  0.21-2.4nm; (0.4-4.5 km)   
Accuracy:		  ?? CEP

NOTES: The rockets can be fired up to  30 knots, sea point - up to 3.
