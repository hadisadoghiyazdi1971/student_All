OVERVIEW: The JYL-1 radar is a  mobile, long-range, phased array 3D surveillance radar operating in the E/F (NATO) or S (IEEE) bands. 

DETAILS: The JYL-1 radar is a long-range tactical air surveillance radar designed to operate in a heavy ECM environment.  It features frequency agility and multiple radar fusion and automatic target tracking.  It also has a high reliability and good mobility.

Specifications:

Frequency: 		 2-4 GHz (E/F Band (NATO), S Band (IEEE)))  
Power output: 		 8 kW(average); 800 kW (peak) 
PRF:			 430 pps (approx)	
Range Resolution:	 80 m (range), 0.25 degree azimuth
