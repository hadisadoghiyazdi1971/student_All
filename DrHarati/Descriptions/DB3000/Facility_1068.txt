OVERVIEW: The TRS 22XX radar is a transportable, 3D E/F-band phased-array radar operating in the NATO "E/F" band.  

DETAILS: The TRS 22XX features enhanced ECCM capablilities over its TRS-2215 and 2230 predecessors, including  pulse-to-pulse or burst-to-burst frequency agility, and automatic extraction of jam
strobes, selection of least jammed frequency, and display of jammed sectors.  The antenna has very low sidelobes.  


Specifications:

Frequency: 		 2-4 GHz (E-F Band (NATO), S Bands (IEEE)))  
Power output: 		 20 kw (mean); 700 kW (peak) 
PRF:			  380 Hz (est., based on TRS 2215)	
Range Resolution:	 120 m (range), 2.5 degrees azimuth 				 
	
On/Off time:		 unknown seconds
Deploy/Stow Time	 < 120 min / < 120 min
Effective Range:	 470 km
			 459 km (vs. 2sq meter-sized target

NOTES: IOC 1989 (approx).  The TRS-22XX is transportable by C-160 and C-130 aircraft.  In use with France, Kuwait, Turkey
