OVERVIEW: The YLC-4 is a 2-D long-range surveillance radar operating in the A (NATO) and VHF (IEEE) bands.

DETAILS:   The YLC-4 is a 2-D long-range surveillance radar intended for early warning. It features pulse-to-pulse and burst-to-burst adaptive frequency agility.  It can synthesize and display data from up to four other radars to provide a unified radar picture.
