OVERVIEW:The Shenyang J-6 (designated F-6 for export versions; NATO reporting name: "Farmer") was the Chinese-built version of the Soviet MiG-19 'Farmer' fighter aircraft,A Soviet second-generation, single-seat, twin jet-engined fighter aircraft.which was the first Soviet production aircraft capable of supersonic speeds in level flight.

DETAILS:Production of the J-6 restarted after new assembly jigs, and other assistance, acquired from the USSR. Similar to MiG-19PF, an all-weather radar-equipped interceptor with two NR-30 30mm cannon. Exported as the F-6A.[

The J-6 was considered "disposable" and was intended to be operated for only 100 flight hours (or approximately 100 sorties) before being overhauled.
