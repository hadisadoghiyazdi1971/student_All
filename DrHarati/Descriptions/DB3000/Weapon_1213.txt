OVERVIEW: The WIZARD (Wideband Zapping Anti-Radar Decoy) Naval Corner Reflector Decoy is a passive RF decoy to distrace or seduce radar guided ASMs with chaff discrimination capablities.

DETAILS: The WIZARD decoys are launched from a ship, float in the air for a specified amount of time, while deflecting incoming missiles aimed at the vessel. The WIZARD remains effective even when it has come to a rest on the water's surface.


NOTES: IOC: 2007. Available in either single or twin corner deflector configurations
