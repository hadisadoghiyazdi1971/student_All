OVERVIEW: The CM-400AKG  is an air-launched, long-range, high-supersonic, solid-fuel AGM using an active radar seeker.  It carries a 150 kg HE or 200 kg penetrator warhead. 

DETAILS: After launch, the missile accelerates to a speed of Mach 3+, first climbing to a high altitude for the cruise phase before diving into the target in the terminal stage.  During the cruise phase it uses inertial and GPS guidance.
