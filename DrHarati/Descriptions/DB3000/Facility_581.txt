OVERVIEW: The TRS-2230  is a fixed/relocatable, 3-D phased array air defense radar operating in the E-F (NATO) or S (IEEE) bands.


DETAILS: The TRS-2230 features high-anti-clutter and anti-jamming capabilities.


Specifications:

Frequency: 		 2-4 GHz (E-F Band (NATO), S Bands (IEEE)))  
Power output: 		 10 kW (average); 700 kW (peak) 
PRF:			 380 Hz	
Range Resolution:	 unknown m (range), 1.5 degrees azimuth 				 
	
On/Off time:		 unknown seconds
Deploy/Stow Time	 < 60 min / < 60 min
Effective Range:	 510 km

NOTES: IOC 1981 (approx).  The TRS-2215 is almost identical to the TRS 2230, the chief difference being the TRS 2230 is the fixed-site/relocatable variant, while the TRS-2215 is road, rail or air transportable. The TRS-2215/30 series has been widely exported.  Customers include Brazil, Cyprus, France, India, Indonesia and Tunisia.
