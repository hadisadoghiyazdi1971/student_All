OVERVIEW: The AAM-1 is a short-range, supersonic, infra-red guided (IR) air-to-air missile with a 4.5 kilogram warhead.   

DETAILS:  The AAM-1 is a virual copy of the AIM-9E Sidewinder.  It is capable of only rear-aspect attacks. Like the AIM-9E, it features an improved seeker with a higher tracking rate.  It also featured cooling for the seeker, which expanded the weapon's acquisition envelope and increased its kill robability.  

Specifications:
Weight:			70 kg 
Length: 		2.60 m 
Diameter: 		127 mm 
Wingspan: 		0.53 m
Warhead:		4.5 kg, blast fragmentation 

PERFORMANCE: 
Operational range: 	2.0 km (1.1 mn) sea level ; 4.6 km (2.5nm) at 30K feet.
Speed: 			2.5 mach
Maneuverability:	12 g
