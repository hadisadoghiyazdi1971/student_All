OVERVIEW: The LSS-1/JY-29 radar is a is truck mounted, high-mobility, low altitude cover, 2D gap-filling tactical radar operating in the D (NATO) or L (IEEE) bands. 

DETAILS: The LSS-1/JY-29 is designed for use within either a national air defence network or for tactical use in mobile warfare.  It is capable of monitoring over 72 tracks.

Specifications:

Power output: 		 unknown kW(average); unknown kW (peak) 
PRF:			 830 pps (approx)	
Range Resolution:	 200 m (range), 1 degree azimuth 					
On/Off time:		 30 seconds

Detection Range:
80% Pd, 2 m2 RCS:	 200 km
