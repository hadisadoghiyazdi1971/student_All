OVERVIEW: The GBU-15(V)1C/B CWW (Cruciform Wing Weapon) is a unpowered glide bomb.


DETAILS: The basic GBU-15 configuration consists of a bolt-on guidance/fin section and a cruciform wing group and data link attached to a Mk 84 bomb.  The GBU-15(V)1C/B CWW guidance section uses a electro-optical sensor for guidance.  It also incorporates a GPS receiver,  giving the weapon an all-weather capability. The weapon can be launched at low altitude.


NOTES:  Does not require target acquisition before release.
