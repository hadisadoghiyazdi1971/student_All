OVERVIEW: The Dagger is a short-range, mobile  3-D target acquisition and surveillance radar operating in the J-band.  

DETAILS: The Dagger multi-beam high resolution 3D radar.  Weapon systems associated with the Dagger are the Rapier SAM.
