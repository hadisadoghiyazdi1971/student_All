OVERVIEW: The OFAB-100-120 fragmentation bomb is a freefall bomb with a high explosive filler.


DETAILS: The OFAB-250-270 fragmentation bomb can be released at 500-1,500 km/hr speed and up to 500-15,000 meters altitude. It has a lethal radius of 25-160 meters against unarmored targets and 10-47 meters against lightly armored targets.


Weight: 	 123 kg
Filling Weight:	  46 kg (TNT equivalent)



NOTES:  The OFAB-250-270 is designed for use against lightly or unarmored targets. 


SOURCES:  Bazalt, "SRPE Bazalt," pg. 22, https://missiles2go.files.wordpress.com/2012/02/bazalt_08_.pdf
