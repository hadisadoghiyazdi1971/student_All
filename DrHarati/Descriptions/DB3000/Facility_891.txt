OVERVIEW: Long range, fixed, nodding height finder radar. 

DETAILS: Height finding on aircraft at long, medium or short range. 

NOTES: The HF 200's (along with the AN/FPS-6) were also known as "Nodding Horrors" and they spewed hydraulic oil everywhere.
