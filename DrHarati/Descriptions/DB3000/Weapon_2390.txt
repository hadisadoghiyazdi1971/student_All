OVERVIEW:  The C-704 aka Nasr is air-or-surface launched, sea-skimming, short-to-medium range, radar guided, solid fuel, anti-ship missile. It has a 130 kg AP warhead.

DETAILS:  The C-704 lightweight anti-ship missile was developed for use against small-to-medium sized naval vessels.  

After launch, the missile is initially guided by inertial navigation system, switching to monopulse active radar guidance during the terminal phase.  The C-704 uses a radar altimeter to cruise at 20 meters, transitioning to 5-7 meters altitude during the terminal phase. The radar seeker has an effective range of approximately 8 nm (15 km).  The C-704 is effective up to Sea State 4.


Specifications:

Weight: 		 360 kg 
Length: 		 3.284 m 
Diameter: 		 280 mm (body)  
Wingspan: 		 1.018 m 
Speed:			 0.8 mach 
Range:			 2.7-20.5 nm (5-38 km)

NOTES: IOC 2006 (approx). Subsequent versions have been equipped with IR and EO seekers. Produced under license by Iran where it is designated 'Nasr'.
