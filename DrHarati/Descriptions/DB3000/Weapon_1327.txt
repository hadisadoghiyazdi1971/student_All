OVERVIEW:  The RB-70 Mk 1  is a short-range, laser beam riding air defense missile, with a  1.1kg warhead using a proximity fuze.

DETAILS: The RB 70 missile uses a laser beam detector mounted at the back of the missile which detects the laser guidance beam. The outputs from the laser beam detectors in the missile tail  are used by an onboard processor to generate the steering and course correction signals to the missile control fins.
