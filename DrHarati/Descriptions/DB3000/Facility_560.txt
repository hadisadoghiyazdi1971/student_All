OVERVIEW: The AN/TPS-59 is a Tactical Ballistic Missile Defense, Early Warning and Situational Awareness Radar System. 

DETAILS: The AN/TPS-59AN/TPS-59 is designed to operate with Patriot or Hawk missile batteries, and naval AEGIS systems. Its power and range even make it suitable for ballistic missile defense roles. 

NOTES: The AN/TPS-59 was used by the US Marines from 1986-1996.
