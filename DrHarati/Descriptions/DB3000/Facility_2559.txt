OVERVIEW: The Martello S-613 is a transportable  height finder radar  operating in NATO "G" band (IEEE "C" Band). 

DETAILS: 

Specifications:

Frequency			5.3-5.4 GHz  (C band (NATO),  C Band (IEEE))
Power output: 			 unknown (average); 1 MW (peak)
PRF:				300 Hz
Range Resolution:		unknown (range)
 				unknowndegrees (azimuth) 
				
On/Off time: 			unknown min. 
Deploy/Stow time: 		unknown min (approx)
Maximum Range:			150 nm (instrumented range)
				Detection range (small aircraft): 500 km (S723A); 425 km (S723C)
