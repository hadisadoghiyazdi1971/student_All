OVERVIEW: The TRS 3405 is a Coast Guard radar designed for surveillance of shipping traffic in straits, harbor entrances and ports operating in the X-Band.

DETAILS: The TRS 3405 is designed to provide high performance in range, resolution and accuracy and protection against the various detection impairments encountered in conditions of sea and rain clutter and propagation loss in rain and heavy fog.
