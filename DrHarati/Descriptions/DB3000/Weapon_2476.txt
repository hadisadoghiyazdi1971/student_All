OVERVIEW: The AN/SSQ-86  Data Link Communications (DLC)  is an "A-size" air-dropped, one way acoustic communications buoy designed for communications with submarines.
 

DETAILS:  The AN/SSQ-86 DLC sonobuoy is an  communication buoy designed to transmit a preprogrammed message to a submerged submarine.
