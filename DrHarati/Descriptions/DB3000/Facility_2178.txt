OVERVIEW: The Vostok-E is a mobile, active array, 2-D early warning radar operating in the VHF band.

DETAILS:  The system is reported to be effective against stealth aircraft.  It is capable of tracking approximately 120 targets. Its ECCM capabilities include a wide  dynamic range, pulse-to-pulse frequency hopping and active noise jamming compensation. Low peak power is used to minimize detection range against hostile ESM

Specifications:

Frequency			150-200 MHz (VHF Band)
Power output: 			14kW (average)
PRF:				400, 800 Hz
Range Resolution:		200 m (range), 5.5 degrees (azimuth)
On/Off time: 			< 5 min. 
Deploy/Stow time: 		6 min
Effective Range (Pd> 0.5):	255 km (max)(vs. B-52 sized target in jammed environment)
				150 km (vs. F-14 sized target in jammed environment)
				57nm ( vs. F-117A in jammed environment)
 

NOTES: IOC 2013. Export version of the Vostok-D.  Intended as a replacement for the SPOON REST D (P-18) series of radars.
