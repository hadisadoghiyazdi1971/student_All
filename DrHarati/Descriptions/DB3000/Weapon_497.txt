OVERVIEW: The CBU-33/A is an unguided, free-falling cluster bomb designed for attacking area targets with anti-armor munitions.
 
DETAILS:  The CBU-33/A (Cluster Bomb Unit-33) is an unguided, aircraft delivered bomb.   It consists of a CBU-58 dispenser unit containing a payload of 30 BLU-45/B anti-armor bomblets.
