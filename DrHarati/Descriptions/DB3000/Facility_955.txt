OVERVIEW: The AN/TPS-64 is a transportable, long range, tactical 3-D radar operating in the E/F Band.

DETAILS:  The AN/TPS-64 was designed to operate as the 3D radar sensor for the marine tactical data system.  It also provides two positions for operators to vector interceptor aircraft to assigned targets. Automatic target detection, clutter elimination, and IFF are included.

NOTES:  Transportable by truck, helo or aircraft.  The AN/TPS-64 is based on the AN/TPS-32, adapted for use in the NATO Air Defense Ground Environment (NADGE) system.  Initial IOC of the TPS-32 was in 1969.
