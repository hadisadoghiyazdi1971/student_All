OVERVIEW: The Apache (Arme Propulsee Antipiste A Charges Ejectables) is a long range,  turbojet-powered,  anti-runway cruise missile utilizing GPS guidance.  

DETAILS:  APACHE carries ten 52 kilogram "Kriss" runway-penetrating submunitions.  

NOTES:  IOC in 2001.  Can be launched from Mirage 2000 and Rafale aircraft.
