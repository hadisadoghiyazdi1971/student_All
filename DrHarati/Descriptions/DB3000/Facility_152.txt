OVERVIEW: The AN/TPS-63 is a transportable 2D air surveillance radar, operating in the D-Band. 

DETAILS: The AN/TPS-63 is a two-dimensional radar that is doctrinally employed as a tactical gap-filler or as an early warning system for early deployment into the operational area.  Jamming effects are also reduced by several forms of frequency agility, including pulse-to-pulse, burst, and sector. The AN/TPS-63 also uses a full array of clutter-reduction processing techniques and can initiate up to 600 targets automatically.  

Specifications:

Frequency: 		  1.250-1.350 GHz (D Band (NATO), L Band (IEEE)))  
Power output: 		  3 kW(average); 100 kW (peak) 
PRF:			  774 pps	
Accuracy:		  150 m (range), 0.35 degrees azimuth 					
On/Off time:		  unknown
Deploy/Stow Time	  60 min (approx)/ 30 min
 
Detection Range:
Pd+90%, 1m2 RCS:	207 km

Max Range:		258 km
