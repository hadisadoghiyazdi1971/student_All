OVERVIEW: The PRV-13 radar (NATO name: ODD PAIR) is a height finding radar operating in the  "E" Band.

DETAILS:   Weapon systems associated with the PRB-13 include the SA-5 GAMMON

Specifications:

Frequency: 		2.5-2.7 Ghz (E-Band)
Power output: 		1 kW (average); 1.6 MW (peak)
PRF:			400 Hz
Range Resolution:	unknown					
On/Off time:		unknown
Detection Ranges	
  Fighter size aircraft
	500 m altitude:	unknown 
 	10000m alt.:	310 km

 
NOTES: The smaller antenna is possibly used to minimize sidelobes.
