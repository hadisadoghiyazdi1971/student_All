OVERVIEW: The ADC Mk 2 Mod 0 Torpedo Decoy is a battery-powered expendable sonar countermeasure for use by surface ships and submarines to counter torpedo threats.

DETAILS:   The ADC (Acoustic Device Countermeasure) hovers vertically at a pre-selected depth, using a pressure-controlled motor driving a small, shrouded propeller in the tail of the decoy.  Once deployed, it emits an acoustic signal to decoy torpedoes.

NOTES:  IOC 1978.
