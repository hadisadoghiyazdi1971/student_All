OVERVIEW: The HEN HOUSE "Daugava" 5U83-series radar is a fixed radar operating in the NATO "A" band and designed to provide early warning and tracking in support of antiballistic missile (ABM) deployments.

DETAILS: Daugava introduced a transmitter array designed for the forthcoming Daryal radar system to the Dnepr complex at EW site RO-1. With this configuration, the Daryal array would act as a transmitter, with the Dnepr arrays acting as receivers, to provide increased system performance.
