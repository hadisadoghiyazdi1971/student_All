OVERVIEW: The YLC-2 is an long range, mobile, 3-D active phased array surveillance radar operating in the L band.

DETAILS:  Its ECCM features include frequency diversity.  It reportedly can incorporate information from other radars to establish a local radar net.

Fequency: 					1.240-1.40 GHz (D Band (NATO), L Band (IEEE)))  
Power output: 		 			5.5 kW(average); 85 W (peak) 
PRF:						275-300 pps  (approx)	
Range Resolution:				300 m (range), unknown degrees (azimuth) 					
On/Off time:					unknown seconds
Deploy/Stow Time	 			<30 min /<30 min
Maximum Range:					550 km (instrumented range)				 

Effective Range:				330 km 



NOTES: Units were sold to Pakistan in 2003 and 2006.
