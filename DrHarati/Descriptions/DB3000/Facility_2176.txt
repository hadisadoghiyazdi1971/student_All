OVERVIEW: The Voronezh-DM is a fixed, long-range  early-warning and surveillance  radar, operating in the UHF band, and designed to detect and track a wide range of threats such as ballistic missiles, aircraft and satellites. 

DETAILS:  The Voronezh early warning system is capable of tracking up to 500 objects simultaneously.
