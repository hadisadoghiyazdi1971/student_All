OVERVIEW: The Dubna-class is a single-shaft, diesel-powered tanker.

DETAILS:   The Dubna-class  is a small Finnish-built tankers, with minimal dry cargo/stores transfer capability

One unit, the Sventa was transfered to Ukraine 1997.  The Irkut was stricken in 1996.

NOTES: Displacement: 6,022 standard; 11,140 tons full load
       Speed: 15.5 knts
       Range: 8,200 nm @ 12 knts; 7,000nm @ 16 knts
       Complement: 62 crew 
       In Commission: 1974
       Vessels in class: 4
