OVERVIEW: The AIM-9F Sidewinder is a short-range, supersonic, infra-red guided (IR) air-to-air missile with a 4.5 kilogram warhead.   

DETAILS:  The AIM-9F was the European version of the AIM-9B. The primary difference over the AIM-9B is a more sensitive CO2-based cooler for the seeker head.  The AIM-9F Sidewinder is capable of only rear-aspect attacks.  

Specifications:
Weight:			71.9 kg 
Length: 		2.83 m 
Diameter: 		127 mm 
Wingspan: 		0.53 m
Warhead:		4.5 kg, blast fragmentation 

PERFORMANCE: 
Operational range: 	2.0 km (1.1 mn)
Speed: 			2.5 mach
Maneuverability:	12 g

NOTES: IOC 1969.  Also called the AIM-9B FGW.2.
