OVERVIEW: The SHANGHAI I class (Type 062) is a diesel-powered fast attack craft (PC).

DETAILS:    

Specifications: 

       Displacement:  113 tons (standard);  131 tons  (full load)
       Speed: 30 knts
       Engineering: 4 shafts, 2 diesel engines 
       Range: 700 nm @ 16 knts
       Complement: 34 
       In Commission: 1961 (approx.) 
       Completed: 320  

NOTES: Eight units were transferred to North Vietnam, seven to Tanzania, six to Guinea, twelve to North Korea, twelve to Pakistan, five to Sira Lanka, six to Albania, one to Angola, eight to banladesh, three to Congo and Four to Egypt.
