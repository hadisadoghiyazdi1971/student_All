OVERVIEW: The OFAB-250-270 fragmentation bomb is a freefall bomb with a high explosive filler.


DETAILS: The OFAB-250-270 fragmentation bomb can be released at 500-1,500 km/hr speed and up to 500-12,000 meters altitude. It has a lethal radius of up to 30-240 meters against unarmored targets and 14-80 meters against lightly armored targets.


Weight: 	 268 kg
Filling Weight:	 94 kg (TNT equivalent)



NOTES:  The OFAB-250-270 is designed for use against military and industrial facilities and lightly or unarmored targets. 


SOURCES:  Bazalt, "SRPE Bazalt," pg. 22, https://missiles2go.files.wordpress.com/2012/02/bazalt_08_.pdf
