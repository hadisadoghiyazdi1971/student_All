OVERVIEW: The DR-151 radar is a is truck mounted, high-mobility, low-level surveillance radar, operating in the D (NATO) or L (IEEE) bands. 

DETAILS: The DR-151 is a mobile low-level surveillance D-Band (1200 to 1400 MHz) radar with a range of 45 km. 

Specifications:

NOTES:   The system is mounted on one 10-tonne truck and is equipped with an 18 m extensible antenna mast.
