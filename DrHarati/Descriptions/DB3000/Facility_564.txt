OVERVIEW: The AN/FPS-93A is a General Surveillance Radar used by the U.S. Air Force Air Defense Command.

DETAILS: Earlier version AN/FPS-20 dominated US air surveillance in the 1950's and 1960s.   

NOTES: The AN/FPS-93 was upgraded to 93A version in 1988.
