OVERVIEW: The T-58-class is a two-shaft, diesel-powered radar picket vessel converted from the T-58 minesweeper.

DETAILS:   The T-58-class (Project 963) is a radar picket vessel, possibly intended primarily for a range security role.


NOTES: Displacement: 880 tons standard; 940 tons full load
       Speed: 17 knts
       Range: 2,500 nm @ 13.5 knts
       Complement: 100 crew (approx.)
       In Commission: 1975
       Vessels in class: 3
