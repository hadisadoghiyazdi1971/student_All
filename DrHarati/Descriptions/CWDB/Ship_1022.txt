OVERVIEW: The PEDER SKRAM class frigate is a 2 shaft gas turbine-powered escort vessel.

DETAILS: The PEDER SKRAM class was an indigenous Danish design.

NOTES: Displacement:   2,200  tons standard
       Speed: 28 knts
       Range: 800 nm @28 knts; 2500 nm @18 knts
       Complement: 200
       In Commission:  1966
       Completed:  2 

SOURCES: Gardiner, Robert, and Norman Friedman. Conway's All the World's Fighting Ships, 1947-1982. Annapolis, Md: Naval Institute Press, 1983, pg 14 ;
