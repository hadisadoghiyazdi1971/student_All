OVERVIEW: The HMCS ANNAPOLIS class helicopter destroyer is a 2 shaft steam destroyer.

DETAILS: A evolutionary continuation of the ST LAURENT class, this vessel incorporated  a deck and hanger for the Sea King helicopter.
