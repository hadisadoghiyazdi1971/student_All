OVERVIEW: The Plessey AR-1 is a fixed or mobile, short-range 2-D approach radar operating in the E/F-Band.  

DETAILS:   The AR-1 was designed to meet both military and civil airfield approach control requirements.

NOTES:  IOC: 1959 (approx.).  The AR-1 was designed to meet both military and civil airfield approach control requirements with many different display configurations, utilising the new Mk.5 Fixed Coil Display. The AR- 1 radar became an international success with sales reaching 108 systems, of which 32 were to the U.K. MOD for use on RAF and Royal Naval stations in the UK and overseas.
