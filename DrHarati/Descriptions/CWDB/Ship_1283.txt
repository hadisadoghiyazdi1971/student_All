OVERVIEW: The HMCS MACKENZIE class destroyer is a 2 shaft steam powered destroyer.

DETAILS: The HMCS MACKENZIE  class destroyer was developed from the RESTIGOUCHE class destroyer.  This design improved the habitabilty over the RESTIGOUCHE class.
