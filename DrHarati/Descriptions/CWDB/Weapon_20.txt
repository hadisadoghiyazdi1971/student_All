OVERVIEW: The Mk84 is a unguided freefall bomb.


DETAILS:  The MK84 is a 2000 pound bomb.


Weight: 	 895-945 kg
Filling Weight:	  429 kg (Tritonal, Comp H6)



NOTES:  The Mk84 has a  lethal radius of 22 meters against standing personnel, and 7 meters against personnel in foxholes. The baseline Mk84 can penetrate up to 15 inches (381.0 mm) of metal or 11 ft (3.4 m) of concrete, depending on the height from which it is dropped.
