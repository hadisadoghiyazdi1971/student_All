OVERVIEW: The AN/ALQ-101 is an external pod mounted, active noise and repeater jammer.

DETAILS:  The AN/ALQ-101 noise and repeater jammer. AN/ALQ-101 (V)4,  covered the 2 to 20 GHz frequency range.  The AN/ALQ-101 (V)8  is the most numerous unit in service. This version  uses a gondola layout, with a trough compartment underneath the main body that increases the available volume. 

NOTES: Used by Germany, Israel and the UK and USA.
