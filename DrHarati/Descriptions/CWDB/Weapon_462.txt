OVERVIEW:  The SA-2b GUIDELINE mod 1 (S-75M Dvina V-750V 11D) is a two stage, solid and liquid fuel, medium range, medium-to-high altitude, radio command guided, surface to air missile equipped with a 190 kg warhead using proximity, contact, and command fuzing.

DETAILS:  After launch, the SA-2b is guided to the target using command guidance utilizing one of three C-band UHF channels. The missile goes ballistic if it does not acquire the guidance beam within six seconds after launch. The warhead has a lethal radius against fighter-sized targets of about 65 m (213 ft) at lower altitudes, and up to  250 m (820 ft) at high altitude. The missile itself is accurate to approximately 75 m (246 ft).


Specifications: 
Weight: 		2,287 kg 
Length: 		10.8 m (with booster)
Diameter: 		0.50 m (missile), 0.65 (booster)
Wingspan: 		2.5 m (booster)
Speed:			3.5 mach
Range:			5.4-18.4 nm (10-34 km)
Altitude		1,640-98,425 ft (500-30,000 m)   
Reload time:		10 minutes
Max target speed: 	594 knts (1,100 km/h)	


NOTES:  IOC:  1958.  Beginning in 1960, was exported to the Warsaw Pack countries.
