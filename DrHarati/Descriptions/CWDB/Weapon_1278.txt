OVERVIEW: The AN/SSQ-41A Jezebel LOFAR  (LOw Frequency Analysis and Ranging) is an A-size omnidirectional sonobuoy.

DETAILS:  The AN/SSQ-41A uses a line hydrophone operating between 10 Hz-20kHz at a depth of 60 or 300 feet and a lifetime of up to 8 hours.


NOTES: IOC in 1963.  Can be launched from an aircraft traveling at up to 370 knots and up to 30,000 feet.
