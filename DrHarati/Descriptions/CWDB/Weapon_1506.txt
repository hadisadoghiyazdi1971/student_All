OVERVIEW: The FAB-1000M (Fugasnaya Aviabomba) is a general purpose (GP) low-drag freefall bomb with a high explosive filler.
 
DETAILS:  The FAB series of general purpose bombs can be released at between 500 meters-12,000 meters altitude and at speeds from 500-1100 km/hour. 

Weight: 	1033 kg
Filling Weight:	 476 kg
 

NOTES:  IOC 1962.
