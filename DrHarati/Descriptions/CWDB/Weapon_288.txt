OVERVIEW: The AN/ALQ-87 is an external pod mounted, Barrage Jamming pod.

DETAILS:  The AN/ALQ-87  is a barrage jamming pod that operatats in the A-H bands (1-8GHz).   


NOTES: The AN/ALQ-87 was used by USAF F-111, F-4, F-100, F-101, F-105, AC-130A, OV-1Ds. It was retired in the mid-1970's.
