OVERVIEW: The GLENTEN class torpedo boat is a 3 shaft Diesel-powered torpedo boat.

DETAILS: The GLENTEN class are former German Kriegsmarine Schnellboote.  

NOTES: Displacement:   112-21  tons standard
       Speed: 39-42 knts
       Range: 700 nm @ 30 knts
       Complement: 24
       In Commission:  1951-65
       Completed:  12 

SOURCES: Gardiner, Robert, and Norman Friedman. Conway's All the World's Fighting Ships, 1947-1982. Annapolis, Md: Naval Institute Press, 1983, pg 16 ;
