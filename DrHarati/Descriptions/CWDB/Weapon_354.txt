OVERVIEW: The AN/ALQ-119 is an external pod mounted, active, dual-mode noise and deception jammer.

DETAILS:  The AN/ALQ-119 is follow on from the AN/ALQ-101 noise and repeater jammer. The AN/ALQ-119 covered the E-J bands (approx. 2 to 20 GHz).  

NOTES: First combat use in 1972.  Used by Germany, Japan and Turkey, Egypt, Israel and the USA.  The AN/ALQ-119 / AN/ALR-46 combination was called Compass Tie.
