OVERVIEW: The AS-3 KANGAROO (Kh-20M) is a liquid-fuel, turbojet-powered, air-to-surface, supersonic nuclear-armed cruise missile.

DETAILS: The AS-3 KANGAROO (Kh-20M) used inertial autopilot with command guidance.  It has a CEP of 150 feet when used in an anti-ship role and a CEP of 1 to 3 nm when used against land targets. 

NOTES:    IOC 1960. Used by the Soviet Union.
