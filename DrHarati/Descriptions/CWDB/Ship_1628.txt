OVERVIEW: The FLETCHER class DD is a twin screw, steam-powered destroyer.

DETAILS:   An all-gun vessel, the FLETCHER class DDs were active in various navies until 2001. 

NOTES: Displacement: 2050 tons standard, 3050 full load
       Speed: 34 knts
       Range:  6000 nm @ 15 knts;  
       Complement: 250 (peacetime) 
       In Commission: 1942- 
       Completed: 175  	  

SOURCES: Blackman, Raymond V. B. Jane's Fighting Ships, 1967-68, 396. New York: McGraw-Hill, 1967.
